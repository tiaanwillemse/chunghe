align-items: flex-start | flex-end | baseline | stretch

the flex-start, flex-end, and center values are straightforward. stretch is also fairly simple: it causes Flex Items to be stretched out from the Cross Start to the Cross End. baseline causes Flex Items to be aligned along their baseline. Baselines are calculated based on the content of the Flex Items.
