Clipping to a Path

When you create an SVG document, you establish its viewport by specifying the width and height of the area you're interested in. This automatically becomes your clipping area; anything drawn outside these limits will not be displayed. You can establish a clipping area of your own with the element. Here's the simplest case: establishing a rectangular clip path. Inside the element will be the we wish to clip to.
